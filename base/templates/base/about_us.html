About Us

UniSphere is a global community platform created to support international students as they navigate academic life, housing, career opportunities, and cultural integration. With features like personalized profiles, group discovery, events/organizations, housing search, and messaging, UniSphere helps students feel connected, informed, and empowered from day one.

Our Mission

To connect and uplift international students by providing inclusive tools and a welcoming platform to thrive in their academic and social journeys.
